Target Audience Description

The audience for the site will involve both the Montevideo business owners and those who are interested in founding more information about the city and its attractions.

Personas

First Persona

David is a 28-year-old man who, after graduating from college with a degree in business administration, took over a manager position alongside his older brother in their family's restaurant. He wants to increase the restaurant's popularity and make it more attractive to tourists, because of that and his wish to increment their network he accesses through his computer the website twice a week.

Second Persona

Mary is a 46 years old mother of two. She and her husband are planning to have a vacation with their family in celebration of her recent promotion. Since graduating from college with a degree in accounting she hasn't had much time to travel, so she finds herself looking on her phone for a place where she can feel calm but that can also her children and husband can find entertaining.

Scenarios

    First Scenario

  • A person planning to travel to Uruguay searches for the main attractions in the country's capital.

    • Second Scenario

  • One of the business owners searches for other companies to associate with on the Directory page.

    • Third Scenario

  • Someone who already visited the country in the past searches for information in the Discover section of the page to share with his family.

    • Fourth Scenario

  • A small business looks for the benefits to join the chamber on the Join page.

    • Fifth Scenario

  • A traveler has questions about the chamber and wants to contact its members to receive answers, he does that by using the Contact page.
